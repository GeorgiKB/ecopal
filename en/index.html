The Advantages of Eco Pallets

Our sunflower husk pallets offer real advantages over traditional wooden pallets.

No ISPM-15 Treatment

Exempt from ISPM-15 requirements — no heat treatment or fumigation needed for international export.

No Mold or Pests

Moisture-resistant structure prevents mold growth. Does not attract insects or harbor pathogens.

Lightweight — Only 15 kg

At 15 kg you save significantly on transport costs compared to wooden pallets at 22–25 kg.

Food Safe

Made from 100% natural materials without toxic additives. Safe for transporting food products.

Up to 60% Space Savings

The compact design allows up to 60% more efficient use of warehouse and transport space.

100% Eco-Friendly

Made from agricultural waste — sunflower husk. Fully biodegradable with a minimal carbon footprint.

About Us

Innovation from Nature

EcoPal is a Bulgarian manufacturer of innovative pallets from sunflower husk — agricultural waste transformed into a sustainable logistics product with high added value.

Our pallets contain 95% sunflower biomass and 5% organic binder. The result is a product lighter and stronger than wood, with better environmental credentials and no need for costly phytosanitary treatment when exporting.
